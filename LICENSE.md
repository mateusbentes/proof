## Why Dual Licensing?

### MIT License (Frontend)
- **Permissive**: Minimal restrictions
- **Commercial**: Can be used commercially
- **Simple**: Easy to understand
- **Community**: Encourages contributions
- **Best for**: Client-side code, UI libraries

### Apache 2.0 License (Backend)
- **Patent Protection**: Includes patent grant
- **Explicit**: Clear terms and conditions
- **Professional**: Widely used in enterprise
- **Compatibility**: Works well with other licenses
- **Best for**: Server code, infrastructure
